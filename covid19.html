Covid-19 Measures

We are taking every measure to ensure that your time at Trent Valley Lanes and Sandy Beach are Safe and Enjoyable! Below are those listed measures:
  • Everyone is required to wear a mask to enter/exit and while moving around within Trent Valley Lanes.
  • Mask removal is permitted while in your assigned bowling area, or while seated at a table in the lounge, or seated at a table on the patio. Masks must be worn at all times when you leave your assigned bowling area, stand up from your lounge or patio table, or cannot maintain at least 2 meters from anyone that is not part of your social circle.
  • Washroom facilities are available but donâ€™t forget to wear your mask and wash your hands.
  • Hand sanitizer is available throughout the facilities, and soap and water in each of the bathrooms.
  • Lane areas, tables, and house bowling balls are sanitized upon departure of each group of bowlers.
  • Floor markings have been added to help maintain safe distancing. This includes no hanging out around any of the counters, with the exception of getting shoes or paying your bill.
  • Bowlers will be assigned 2 lanes per Social Circle. As Social Circles can include up to 10 people, bowlers are encouraged to bowl within their Social Circles to give others an opportunity to bowl as we only have enough lanes to accommodate 3 Social Circles. Each Social Circle is a maximum of 10 people, bowling or not.
