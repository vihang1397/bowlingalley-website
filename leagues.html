Youth Bowl Canada Bowlers:

The start of the YBC League season will be September 12, 2020. The alley will open at 9:30am. Registration will be done prior to bowling on the day we start. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out by phone or email. Please check out our Covid-19 Tab to see what we are doing to ensure you and your families safety during your visit.

KIDS BOWL FREE!

Although this year’s Kids Bowl Free program was off to a late start due to Covid, it is now up and running for you and your kids bowling enjoyment. Visit www.kidsbowlfree.com to sign up. Here are the times available for Kids Bowl Free that will be in effect until September 7, 2020:

  • Monday ~ Closed
  • Tuesday ~ Closed
  • Wednesday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Thursday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Friday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Saturday ~ Not Available
  • Sunday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
Lane availability is reduced due to the province’s social distancing mandate so we encourage you to call in advance for a reservation. Walk-ins will be accepted based on lane availability. Trent Valley Lanes is prepared to extend the Kids Bowl Free program beyond September 7, 2020 if there are any delays in the start of the school year.
