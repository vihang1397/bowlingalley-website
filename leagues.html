KIDS BOWL FREE!

Although this year’s Kids Bowl Free program was off to a late start due to Covid, it is now up and running for you and your kids bowling enjoyment. Visit www.kidsbowlfree.com to sign up. Here are the times available for Kids Bowl Free that will be in effect until Sept 7, 2020:

  • Monday ~ Closed
  • Tuesday ~ Closed

  • Thursday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm
  • Friday ~ 11:00am – 5:00pm

Trent Valley Lanes is prepared to extend the Kids Bowl Free program beyond 07 September 2020 if there are any delays in the start of the school year.
