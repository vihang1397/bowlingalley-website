ABOUT US

There's nothing quite like Trent Valley Lanes, when you want to experience a real retro-style 50's 5 pin bowling alley for the whole family, in downtown Campbellford. Featuring 8 fully air-conditioned bowling lanes, a licensed bar, rock n bowl & live DJ, and 5 TVs to catch all the sports you and your friends could want. Our lounge features 1 big screen so you can experience the Stanley Cup, Super Bowl, Fight Night and more in style! We offer a full menu and drink features daily. If you're looking for a kids party venue, turn to TVL for a bowling party that kids will be talking about for weeks! Join us! We are so much more than a bowling alley!
